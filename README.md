🌩️ Weatheron — Live Weather Radar

A real-time weather intelligence platform with AI-powered disaster risk prediction and emergency assistance.

🌐 Live Demo
👉 https://pritammondal2006.github.io/Weatheron/


📖 About
Weatheron is a full-featured, browser-based weather dashboard that combines live radar data, AI forecasting, air quality monitoring, and an intelligent emergency assistant — all in one sleek interface.
Built for real-world utility, Weatheron goes beyond just showing temperature. It helps users understand environmental risk, locate nearby hospitals, and get instant AI-driven guidance during emergencies.

✨ Features

🌤️ Live Weather

Real-time weather conditions for your current location
Temperature, humidity, UV index, wind speed, and more
Multi-day forecast with precipitation probability

💨 Air Quality Index (AQI)

Live AQI monitoring with detailed pollutant breakdown
PM2.5, PM10, O₃ (ozone), NO₂ readings
Color-coded risk levels for at-a-glance safety assessment

🤖 AI Forecast & Risk Prediction

AI-generated weather narratives powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash
Disaster risk analysis for your map location — floods, heatwaves, storms, and more
One-click "Analyze Now" for instant AI risk summaries

💬 Emergency Assistant (Chatbot)

24/7 intelligent chat assistant for emergency guidance
Voice input support 🎤
Quick-action buttons: Hospital 🏥 | Heatwave 🔥 | Flood 🌊 | Emergency 🚨

📍 Interactive Map

Location-aware radar map
Pinpoint any location for localized risk analysis  
Hospital locator integrated into the map view
